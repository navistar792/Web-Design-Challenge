City Latitude vs. Cloudiness

Cloudiness and Latitude

This plot examines whether there is a relationship between latitude and cloudiness.

This plot is a good example of displaying no relationship between two variables. There appears to be no trend or pattern in the data.

Other interesting observations from the plot include the number of cities with no cloudiness. Is this just a result of short-term weather patterns, or a more typical trend?

An interesting follow-up study would be to examine whether there is a relationship between humidity and cloudiness. Are high humidity countries also more likely to be cloudy?
