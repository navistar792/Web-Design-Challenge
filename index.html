What's the weather today?

Weather is one of the most commonly discussed topics. Many people watch the weather, and discuss it with others on social media. Why is weather so interesting?

This site seeks to examine several aspects of global weather, including:

  • Humidity
  • Temperature
  • Cloudiness
  • Wind Speed

The four analyses examine whether there is a relationship between latitude and the four variables mentioned above.

Before viewing these analyses, consider the following questions:

  1. How do you expect temperature to change at different latitudes?
  2. Do you think wind speed might be different at different latitudes? Where?
  3. Do you think humidity might be different in different parts of the globe? Where?
  4. Do you think that cloudiness differs in different parts of the globe? Where?
